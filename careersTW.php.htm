Careers at AES motomation - Taiwan

Repair / Field Service Technicians / Engineers

Position Description:

  • Provide tech support to customers in the pre-sales and post-sales phase
  • Trouble shoot field problems remotely by identifying root cause problems, communicating them to the technical support teams and providing solutions to the customers
  • Trouble shoot field problems on site; Identify root cause problems and make repairs
  • Participate in in-house repairs and refurbishments
  • Participation in our 24/7 phone and on-site support
  • Support spare parts business
  • Provide customer training
  • Applications Engineering support (if interested and capable)
  • Our new team members will be trained extensively, starting out as Service Technicians with the opportunity to manage our support program later on

Requirements:

  • Excellent mechanical, electrical and software use aptitude
  • Experience working with semiconductor equipment
  • Experience with semiconductor automation (robots) preferred, but will train correct candidate
  • Strong communication skills in an international environment, exchanging information with customers, suppliers and colleagues
  • Ability to work independently as well as in a team
  • Must be able to travel up to 50% at times
  • Must be willing to participate in various areas in support of the business

AES motomation provides excellent opportunity for personal growth and development, and competitive salary & benefits. No Relocation Assistance available.
