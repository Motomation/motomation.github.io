integrated Front End Module

  • Concept
    • Turn-key Front End Handling unit incorporated into Process Tool
    • Handling Robot, Load Ports and Accessories integrated into a single solution
  • Advantages
    • Cost Savings - iFEM and process tool share same mini-environment, safety features and offer a reduced footprint.
    • Highly Flexible Design - which easily meets customer requirements.
    • High Reliability and Performance - using Sinfonia Technology's & Sankyo's standard handling products.

We now offer a turn-key front-end handling solution for process equipment that utilizes Sinfonia Technology's load ports as well as Sankyo robotics and accessories. Both companies provide a rich portfolio of handling solutions which can be integrated into our iFEM, offering cost savings, common safety features, high reliability and a multitude of configurations, all while reducing your front-end footprint.
