Privacy Policy

Use and transmission of your personal data, specific purpose

We are delighted that you are visiting our website. The protection of your personal data is a special concern for us and we pay close attention to this matter. You can generally visit our website without providing any personal data. There are occasions when we need your personal data, e.g. to send you requested information, offers, when processing service requests or job applications, and for the use of specific information such as technical details.

When submitting applications, we are entitled to use the data within the AES motomation Group. Unless you instruct us otherwise, we keep the application documents for future consideration.

AES motomation is a global company. To the extent permitted and required your personal data will be transmitted in connection with the performance of our contractual services or for the purpose of processing a request in other countries within the EU or also to our branch offices or to contractual partners outside the EU and used, stored and processed there, as necessary. We have taken care, by maintaining high security standards and by concluding agreements for data processing in accordance with the standard clauses of the European Union (2010), to ensure that your data is protected outside the EU to the same extent as in Germany. This is especially true for the transmission to our foreign subsidiaries. But even with contractual partners and external service providers, we have ensured through the conclusion of agreements for commissioned data processing that your personal data will be as protected as possible, even if data is transferred to third parties to fulfill our contractual obligations or to answer your request.

Personal data, to the extent it is required to process your request and/or fulfill the contractual services, will be provided at the required level to third parties (service providers, contractual partners) that we use to fulfill the contractual services, as necessary, for the required time. For any questions concerning information, offers or services, we are entitled to pass on the information within the AES motomation Group or to those business partners responsible for these activities for the purpose of processing.

In addition to following up on a request or order transaction, we may contact you for the purpose of quality control or for references to other products or services of our company. Your data will not be transmitted to third parties for their promotional purposes.

Links

When you click on links of external Internet sites you leave the AES motomation website. The linked websites are beyond our control. We take no responsibility for the content and compliance with data protection regulations on these websites.

We don’t collect data for statistical analysis.

Social Networks

Our website neither include links to social media websites nor use plug-ins of these websites.

Revocation and right to information

You can revoke at any time in writing the consent that you gave to store and use your personal data. You can receive at any time, upon request and free of charge, information about your stored data. You have the right to revise, delete or block incorrect and, possibly, correct data. Deletion of data may conflict under certain circumstances with statutory provisions, especially those of the tax code, and thus in terms of data required for accounting and bookkeeping purposes.
